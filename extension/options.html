OpenCode Annotation

Send element annotations to your OpenCode agent. Text and metadata only.

Shortcuts: Alt+A to pick an element, Alt+Shift+A to toggle the annotation list. If they do nothing after an unpacked reload, set them at chrome://extensions/shortcuts.

When OpenCode runs on a remote host, forward the port from your desktop and point this at the local end:

ssh -N -L 39517:127.0.0.1:39517 you@host
